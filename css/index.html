Cognitive behavioral therapy

In our psychotherapy offices, we work in the cognitive-behavioral approach. This is a form of psychotherapy that focuses on the impact of the patient's thoughts and beliefs on his or her emotions and behavior.

Many studies have shown that cognitive-behavioral therapy can significantly reduce the symptoms of mental disorders such as anxiety, depression, PTSD and ADHD. This therapeutic trend is also used in marital problems, group therapy and addiction treatment.
